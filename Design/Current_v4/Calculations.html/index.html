Calculations
Notation The notation below is a combination of traditional notations and my own ‘pseudo notation’
Allele Frequencies: (range 0.0–1.0):
p Frequency of the A allele, p(#) = frequency in generation #, etc
q Frequency of the B allele
r Frequency of the C allele
Note: p & q are the traditional notations for frequency of 2 alleles; I've added r for the 3rd.
Suggest we use FreqA, FreqB, FreqC in coding.
With all of these we could use parentheses to indicate generation, e.g. FreqA(1) is frequency of A allele in generation 1
Genotype Frequencies: FreqAA, FreqAB, FreqAC, FreqBC, FreqBB, FreqCC
Survival rates: (range 0.0–1.0):
SurvR8AA, SurvR8AB, SurvR8AC, SurvR8BC, SurvR8BB, SurvR8CC
Reproductive rates: (range 0.0–10.0):
RepR8AA, RepR8AB … RepR8CC
Absolute Fitnesses: (range 0.0–10.0): Note: W, or ω, is traditional notation for ‘Fitness’ (sometimes absolute & sometimes relative), with Selection coefficient (s) defined as 1 - W
WAbsAA, WAbsAB, … WAbsCC
Relative Fitnesses: (range 0.0–1.0):
WRelAA, WRelAB … WRelCC
Total population size: (range 0–10,000)
NumPop
Genotype numbers: NumAA, NumAB, NumAC, NumBC, NumBB, NumCC
Mutation rates: from A to B and vice versa
MuR8A2B), MuR8B2A … MuR8C2B
Migration rates: (0.0–0.95)
MigR8AA, MigR8AB … MigR8CC
Mating rates: (QUERY: may need another symbol if & is an operator?)
M8R8AA&AA, M8R8AA&AB … M8R8CC&CC
Formulae
Calculation of Allele & Genotype frequencies
Allele Frequencies
2–Allele Case: p + q = 1
Given FreqA, FreqB = 1 - FreqA
3–Allele Case: p + q + r = 1
Given FreqA, FreqB, then FreqC = 1 - (FreqA + FreqB)
Expected freq.s of genotypes (Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium)
2–Allele Case: p^2 + 2pq + q^2 = 1
FreqAA = FreqA^2
FreqBB = FreqB^2
FreqAB = 2*FreqA*FreqB
3–Allele Case: freq.s of genotypes = p^2 + q^2 + r^2+ 2pq + 2pr + 2qr = 1
FreqAA = FreqA^2
FreqBB = FreqB^2
FreqCC = FreqC^2
FreqAB = 2*FreqA*FreqB
FreqAC = 2*FreqA*FreqC
FreqBC = 2*FreqB*FreqC
Allele freq.s given genotype numbers
2–Allele Case: given NumAA + NumAB + NumBB = NumPop
FreqA = (2 * NumAA + NumAB) / (2 * NumPop)
FreqB = (2 * NumBB + NumAB) / (2 * NumPop)
3–Allele Case: given NumAA + NumBB + NumCC + NumAB + NumAC + NumBC = NumPop
FreqA = (2 * NumAA + NumAB + NumAC) / (2 * NumPop)
FreqB = (2 * NumBB + NumAB + NumBC) / (2 * NumPop)
FreqC = (2 * NumCC + NumAC + NumBC) / (2 * NumPop)
Calculation of Fitnesses
W or ω is a traditional notation for ‘Fitness’ (which may be either absolute or relative), with Selection coefficient, s, defined as 1 - W
Calculate Absolute Fitnesses given survival & reproductive rates
WAbsAA = SurvR8AA * RepR8AA
WAbsBB = SurvR8BB * RepR8BB
:
WAbsBC = SurvR8BC * RepR8BC
Calculate Relative Fitnesses given absolute fitnesses
WRelAA = WAbsAA / Maximum(WAbsAA, WAbsBB, WAbsCC, WAbsAB, WAbsAC, WAbsBC)
:
WRelBC = WAbsBC / Maximum(WAbsAA, WAbsBB, WAbsCC, WAbsAB, WAbsAC, WAbsBC)
Calculate Mean Fitness of population
Mean Fitness of a population = average fitness of the whole population = fitness of each genotype multiplied by its frequency. The traditional notation is W(hat),
MeanAbsFitPop = (FreqAA*AbsFitAA) + (FreqBB*AbsFitBB) + (FreqCC*AbsFitCC) + (FreqAB*AbsFitAB) + (FreqAC*AbsFitAC) + (FreqBC*AbsFitBC)
